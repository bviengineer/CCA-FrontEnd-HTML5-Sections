Organizations That Specialize in Extra Cirriular Programming

Kids 4 Coding

Kids 4 Coding/a>Kids 4 coding offers instruction in programming, 3D digital art, robotics, game design and mobile development. Services are after school on an ongoing basis or during the summer as weekly full day sessions or weekly half-day sessions.

Tekkie Uni


Tekki Uni provides online instructor-led extracurricular programming classes that runs for appoximatley 2 hours a week and for approximately 9 months at a time using a project-based style of learning.

iD Tech


ID Tech offers technology instruction to children in a summer cap environment as an all day summer camp, overnight summer camp and several other variations for children of all ages.

Google CS First

Perhaps the most well known technology initiative, Google CS First is a global technology program designed to bring aspiring student developers into the open source arena by working with an open source organization on a project during summer break that results in a winning team.

What Is S.T.E.M.

S.T.E.M is the acronym for Science Technology Engineering Math.
