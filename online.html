Online Resources for Kids Learning How to Code

Code.Org

Code is a non-profit whose goals is to increase access to computer science for minorities and other underrepresented groups. Code.org's intro to CS curriculum is used in a vast amount of schools througout the United States. Code.org is also the organizer of the annual Hour of Code. Particiation in Code.org's programs are at no cost.

Khan Academy

Khan Academy is a non-profit that creates and partners with other organizatoins and universities to create content across an array of subject matters, including computer science. Access to all of Khan Academy's courses, are at no cost.

Scratch by MIT

Creating interactive programs via stories, games and animations, being able to share them with an online community is the method used by Scratch. Scratch was invented by MIT and is widely used.

Scratch, Jr.

Inspired by Scratch, Scratch, Jr. is geared towards children ages 5 - 7 by using blocks that snpa together to create animations, interactive stories and games. Scratch, Jr. is an app that can be downloaded from the Anroid and Apple stores.

What Is S.T.E.M.

S.T.E.M is the acronym for Science Technology Engineering Math.
